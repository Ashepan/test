You are going to love Zimbabwe

Once a place of conflict, Harare has reinvented itself as a peaceful, stable modern city. There has been a surge in the country's fortunes, which have restored a city that was historically a center for trade, culture, and commerce. Today you'll find a chic luxury beach party scene, fine dining, and shopping in a lush and welcoming tropical locale.
